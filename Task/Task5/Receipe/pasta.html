PASTA




Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste

Instructions

Bring a large pot of generously salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente (firm to the bite). Before draining, reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water, then drain the pasta.
While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant and lightly golden, being careful not to burn it.
Add the canned tomatoes (crush whole tomatoes with a wooden spoon or an immersion blender if needed) to the skillet. Stir to combine.
Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly and the flavors meld. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add the cooked, drained penne directly to the sauce in the pan. Toss well to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water to loosen it to your desired consistency.This video provides a great demonstration of the cooking process, from preparing the aromatics to combining the pasta and sauce:
Remove from heat. Stir in fresh parsley or basil, if using, and serve immediately. Garnish with extra herbs and a sprinkle of grated cheese, if desired.
