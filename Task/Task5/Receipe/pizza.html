PIZZA




Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Semolina

Instructions

Preheat your oven to a high temperature, typically 450-500°F (230-260°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven while it heats up for a crispier crust.
Roll or stretch your pizza dough into a thin round or square shape on a lightly floured surface or parchment paper.
Spread a thin, even layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle with cheese, then add your choice of toppings (e.g., pre-cooked meats, vegetables). Avoid overloading the pizza to prevent a soggy base.
Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (onto the hot stone or baking sheet). Bake for approximately 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a minute or two, then slice and serve hot. You can add fresh herbs like basil as a garnish after baking.
