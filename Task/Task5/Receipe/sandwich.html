Sandwich




Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Vegetables

Instructions

Collect two slices of bread, your desired spreads (mayo, mustard, butter, etc.), and fillings (meat, cheese, vegetables).
Spread your chosen condiments evenly on one or both slices of bread.
Layer the meat and/or cheese on one slice of bread. Place heavier items like meat and cheese first, followed by lighter items such as lettuce and tomatoes.
Place the second slice of bread on top of the fillings to complete the sandwich.
Gently press the sandwich together, and cut it in half diagonally if desired.
For a grilled sandwich, butter the outside of the assembled sandwich and cook it in a pan or a sandwich maker until the bread is golden brown and the fillings are warm.
