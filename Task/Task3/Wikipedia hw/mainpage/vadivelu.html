Vadivelu

Vadivelu is one of the most popular and talented comedy actors in Tamil cinema, loved by audiences for his unique expressions, timing, and body language. He was born on September 10, 1960, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Before entering films, Vadivelu worked in small stage shows and gained attention for his natural humor. He made his film debut with En Rasavin Manasile (1991), but his breakthrough came with movies like Kadhalan, Friends, and Winner. Known as “Vaigai Puyal” (the Storm of Vaigai), Vadivelu has become a household name in Tamil Nadu.

His comedy scenes are famous for their catchy dialogues, facial expressions, and timing, which continue to entertain people even years after release. He has acted in hundreds of films alongside leading actors like Rajinikanth, Vijay, Ajith, and Vikram, and has also played lead roles in movies such as Imsai Arasan 23am Pulikesi, Eli, and Tenaliraman. Vadivelu’s comedies often include social messages and human emotions, making them both funny and meaningful.

He has received several Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and Filmfare Awards for Best Comedian. Vadivelu’s contribution to Tamil cinema is immense—his dialogues and expressions have become part of everyday Tamil culture. With his unique style and endless energy, he remains one of the most beloved comedians in Indian cinema.
