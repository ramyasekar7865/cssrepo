Santhanam

Santhanam is a popular Tamil film actor and comedian, known for his witty dialogues, perfect timing, and unique sense of humor. He was born on January 21, 1980, in Pozhichalur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Santhanam began his career in television through the famous comedy show “Lollu Sabha” on Vijay TV, where he recreated popular Tamil films in a funny and creative way. His excellent performance on television gained him wide recognition and led to opportunities in cinema.

Santhanam made his film debut with Manmadhan (2004) and soon became one of the most sought-after comedians in Tamil cinema. He worked with leading actors like Vijay, Ajith, Suriya, and Dhanush, adding humor and energy to every film he appeared in. His comic style often includes quick comebacks, modern slang, and relatable humor, which made him especially popular among the youth. Some of his notable movies include Boss Engira Bhaskaran, Oru Kal Oru Kannadi, Vasuvum Saravananum Onna Padichavanga, and Sakka Podu Podu Raja.

In recent years, Santhanam has successfully transitioned into a lead actor, taking on hero roles in films like Dhilluku Dhuddu, Server Sundaram, Parris Jeyaraj, and Gulu Gulu. Apart from his comedy skills, he is appreciated for his hard work, simplicity, and continuous growth in the film industry. Today, Santhanam is regarded as one of the most influential modern comedians and actors in Tamil cinema.
