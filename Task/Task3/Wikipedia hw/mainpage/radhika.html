Radhika

Radhika Sarathkumar is a popular and respected Indian actress, producer, and television personality who has made a remarkable contribution to Tamil cinema and television. She was born on August 21, 1963, in Colombo, Sri Lanka, to the famous actor M. R. Radha. Radhika made her acting debut at a young age in the film Kizhakke Pogum Rail (1978), directed by Bharathiraja. Her natural acting and expressive eyes quickly earned her fame, and she went on to become one of the most talented actresses of the 1980s and 1990s.

Radhika has acted in more than 300 films across Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, and Kannada languages. She is known for portraying strong female characters with emotion and grace in movies like Keladi Kanmani, Neethana Antha Kuyil, Dharma Durai, Nallavanukku Nallavan, and Jeans. Apart from films, she became a household name through her hit television serials such as Chithi, Annamalai, Selvi, and Vani Rani, where her performances were loved by audiences of all ages.

She is also the founder of the production company Radaan Mediaworks, which produces many successful TV serials and shows. Radhika has received several Filmfare Awards and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards for her outstanding acting. Known for her strong personality, leadership, and professionalism, Radhika Sarathkumar continues to be one of the most influential and respected women in the Tamil film and television industry.
