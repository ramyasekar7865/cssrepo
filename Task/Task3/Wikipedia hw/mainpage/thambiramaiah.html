Thambi Ramaiah

Thambi Ramaiah is a well-known actor, comedian, director, and lyricist in Tamil cinema, admired for his humorous and emotional performances. He was born on June 19, 1956, in Rasipuram, Namakkal District, Tamil Nadu. Before entering films, he worked in various fields and had a strong passion for acting and writing. He began his career in the Tamil film industry as an assistant director and dialogue writer, later becoming a full-time actor. Thambi Ramaiah is known for his natural comedy timing, expressive face, and heartfelt acting that appeal to audiences of all ages.

He has acted in many successful films such as Mynaa, Kumki, Manam Kothi Paravai, Jilla, Komban, and Appa. His performance in Mynaa (2010) earned him the National Film Award for Best Supporting Actor. Apart from acting, he has also directed movies like Manusbirth and written dialogues for several films. Thambi Ramaiah is respected for his humble personality, hard work, and dedication to cinema. With his mix of comedy and emotion, he has become one of the most beloved character artists in Tamil cinema and continues to entertain audiences with his versatile performances.
