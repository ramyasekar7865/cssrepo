Delhi Ganesh

Delhi Ganesh is a senior Tamil film and television actor known for his versatile performances and long-standing contribution to Indian cinema. He was born on August 1, 1944, in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. Before entering films, he worked in the Indian Air Force and later followed his passion for acting by joining a drama troupe in Delhi — which earned him the nickname “Delhi” Ganesh. He made his film debut with Pattina Pravesam (1977), directed by K. Balachander, who gave him many opportunities to showcase his talent.

Over the years, Delhi Ganesh has acted in more than 400 films, playing a wide variety of roles — from comedy and supporting characters to emotional and serious roles. He is known for his natural acting style, clear dialogue delivery, and expressive face. Some of his notable movies include Nayakan, Michael Madana Kamarajan, Avvai Shanmugi, Pammal K. Sambandam, Indian, and Pudhupettai. He has also appeared in many popular Tamil TV serials, earning love and respect from family audiences.

Delhi Ganesh has received several awards and recognition for his contributions to Tamil cinema. Even today, he continues to act in films and television with the same passion and dedication. His career is a great example of hard work, talent, and consistency, making him one of the most respected character actors in South Indian cinema.
