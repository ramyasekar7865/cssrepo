CINEMA

Cinema means the art or business of making movies — using moving images to tell stories, express ideas, or evoke emotions. The word cinema comes from the Greek word “kinema”, meaning movement. It’s also short for cinematograph, an early device used to record and project motion pictures. Cinema is the art and industry of making and showing motion pictures (movies or films). It combines storytelling, acting, photography, sound, and editing to create visual entertainment.

    Main Components of Cinema:

  • SCIPT/STORY - The written idea or narrative.
  • DIRECTION- How the story is visually told.
  • EDITING- Arranging shots to form a complete movie.
  • MUSIC AND SOUND- Adds mood and emotion.
  • PRODUCTION- Costumes, sets, and locations that build the world of the story.

Hero

A hero is the main character in a story or movie who stands for courage, honesty, and determination. In cinema, the hero plays a central role by facing challenges, fighting against evil, and protecting others. A true hero inspires people through his actions, strength, and moral values. In Tamil cinema, heroes like Rajinikanth, Kamal Haasan, Vijay, and Ajith Kumar have become symbols of bravery and hope. The hero’s journey often shows the victory of good over evil, teaching audiences the importance of faith, hard work, and justice.The hero is the main focus of the story — the audience follows their journey. Usually, the conflict of the story is between the hero and the villain.The hero’s growth and victory form the emotional heart of the film

Examples in Tamil Cinema:

  • Rajinikanth- Known for stylish, mass-appeal heroic roles.
  • Kamal Haasan - Plays intelligent, emotional, or patriotic heroes.
  • Vijay- Modern-day action and family-oriented hero.
  • Ajith Kumar- Known for strong, determined, silent hero characters.
  • Surya- Often plays inspiring and socially responsible roles.

Supporting Actors

A supporting actor is a performer who plays secondary roles that help and highlight the main characters in a movie. Their presence strengthens the story and provides emotional balance or humor to the main plot. Supporting actors often play roles like siblings, friends, co-workers, or other important side characters. They may appear in fewer scenes than the hero or heroine, but their performances leave a lasting impact. In Tamil cinema, Samuthirakani, Saranya Ponvannan, Thambi Ramaiah are famous supporting actors who make every scene meaningful and realistic.

A supporting actor is an actor who plays an important secondary role that helps develop the hero’s or heroine’s story. They are not the central focus, but they provide balance, humor, emotion, or guidance to the main characters. Supporting actors make the world of the movie feel fuller and more believable.A supporting actor’s success lies in being natural and convincing — they help the audience connect emotionally with the story while allowing the hero and heroine to shine. Even though they may not have the biggest screen presence, their contribution is essential for storytelling and emotional depth in cinema.


Comedy Actors

A comedy actor (or comedian) is an actor who plays humorous roles in films, theatre, or television, aiming to entertain the audience through comedy — by creating laughter from funny situations, dialogues, or character behavior.A comedy actor is a performer who specializes in making people laugh through their expressions, timing, dialogue delivery, and body language.


Examples in Tamil Cinema:

  • Nagesh - Known for expressive acting and situational comedy.
  • Goundamani - Famous duo known for dialogue-based humor.
  • Vadivelu - Known for facial expressions, body language, and witty one-liners.
  • Vivek- Added social messages within comedy.
  • Santhanam- Modern-day comedy stars mixing humor with heroism.


Charactor Actors

A character actor is an artist who plays strong supporting roles that add depth and meaning to a movie. Unlike heroes or villains, a character actor portrays realistic and memorable characters such as parents, teachers, mentors, or friends. These roles may not always be in the spotlight, but they are essential for making the story believable and emotional. In Tamil cinema, actors like Nassar, Delhi Ganesh, Manorama , Radhika Sarathkumar are well-known character actors who bring life and emotion to their films through their natural performances.


A character actor is an actor who specializes in playing distinctive, realistic, and supporting roles that help define the story’s emotional depth. Unlike a hero or villain, a character actor doesn’t always play the lead, but their presence gives the film authenticity, personality, and heart. Character actors often support the hero’s emotional journey, helping to show family bonds, moral lessons, or life struggles. Their performances may not always earn the spotlight, but they are vital for creating realism and emotional connection in cinema.


Villan Actors

A villain actor is a performer who portrays negative, evil, or opposing roles in films, theatre, or television. They create conflict in the story and make the hero’s journey more intense and meaningful.A villain actor is an actor who specializes in playing the antagonist — the character who opposes the hero or main character in a story.They have a commanding personality that captures audience attention. They Delivers intense or menacing lines powerfully.Their Confident, controlled movements that show authority or danger. They Can show anger, jealousy, cruelty, or even sympathy when needed. A good villain actor makes the audience understand why the character turned bad.
