Surya

Saravanan Sivakumar, popularly known by his stage name Suriya, is one of the most talented and admired actors in Tamil cinema. He was born on July 23, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, to veteran actor Sivakumar and Lakshmi Sivakumar. Suriya is the elder brother of actor Karthi. He began his acting career with the film Nerukku Ner (1997), produced by Mani Ratnam. After a few initial struggles, he gained recognition through movies like Nandha (2001) and Kaakha Kaakha (2003). Known for his natural acting, expressive eyes, and dedication, Suriya has played diverse roles in films such as Ghajini, Vaaranam Aayiram, Singam, 24, Soorarai Pottru, and Jai Bhim.

Apart from being a successful actor, Suriya is also a film producer and philanthropist. Along with his wife, actress Jyothika, he runs the Agaram Foundation, which supports the education of underprivileged children. Suriya has received several awards, including Filmfare Awards, Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, and National Awards. His portrayal of real-life roles and socially relevant characters has made him one of the most respected actors in Indian cinema. Suriya is admired not only for his acting skills but also for his humility, discipline, and social responsibility, making him a true role model for today’s generation.
