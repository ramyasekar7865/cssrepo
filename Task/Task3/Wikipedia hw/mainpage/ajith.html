Ajith

Ajith Kumar, popularly known as Thala Ajith, is one of the most respected and versatile actors in Tamil cinema. He was born on May 1, 1971, in Secunderabad, Telangana, and was raised in Chennai. Before entering films, Ajith worked as a mechanic and was also a professional car racer, which shows his passion for automobiles and sports. He began his acting career with small roles and gained recognition with the film Aasai (1995). His breakthrough came with movies like Kadhal Kottai (1996), Vaali (1999), and Mugavaree (2000). Over the years, Ajith became known for his strong screen presence, stylish looks, and natural acting. He has successfully performed in a wide range of roles — from romantic hero to action star — in hit films like Dheena, Varalaru, Billa, Mankatha, Veeram, Viswasam, and Valimai.

Fans lovingly call him “Thala”, meaning “leader,” for his simplicity, discipline, and leadership qualities. Ajith is also known for living a low-profile and humble life, avoiding public functions and focusing on his craft. Apart from acting, he has represented India in motor racing championships and has a keen interest in aeromodelling and photography. Despite his massive stardom, he is admired for his self-respect, hard work, and honesty. Ajith Kumar remains an inspiration to many young people for his determination and down-to-earth nature, making him one of the most respected and admired icons in Tamil cinema.
