VIJAY

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Actor Vijay, is one of the leading stars in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay began his acting career as a child artist and made his debut as a hero in the film Naalaiya Theerpu (1992). Over the years, he became one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in South India. Known as “Thalapathy” by his fans, Vijay is admired for his energetic screen presence, stylish action scenes, and dance performances. Some of his major hit movies include Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, Master, Leo, and Kaththi. Apart from acting, Vijay is also known for his simplicity, charitable activities, and strong fan following. His dedication and consistent performances have made him one of the most influential personalities in Indian cinema.

Apart from acting, Vijay is also a playback singer and has sung several hit songs in his films. He is loved by millions of fans who call him “Thalapathy,” meaning “Commander.” Beyond cinema, Vijay is known for his humble nature and social service through the Vijay Makkal Iyakkam, which helps in education, disaster relief, and welfare activities. He has received several awards, including Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and SIIMA Awards, and has been featured multiple times in Forbes India’s Celebrity 100 list. With his growing influence and positive public image, Vijay continues to be a symbol of hard work, dedication, and inspiration in Indian cinema.
